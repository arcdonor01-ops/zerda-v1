How to Choose Your Platform

1

Assess Your Level

Be honest about your current skill level. Beginners should start with more accessible platforms like YesWeHack or Bugcrowd, while experienced hunters can aim for HackerOne's private programs.

2

Consider Your Location

European hunters might prefer Intigriti or YesWeHack for GDPR compliance and local programs. US-based hunters often find more opportunities on HackerOne and Bugcrowd.

3

Define Your Goals

Looking for high payouts? Try HackerOne. Want to learn and grow? Consider platforms with educational focus like Intigriti. Need quick wins? Start with beginner-friendly options.

4

Start Small

Don't try to master all platforms at once. Pick one or two that match your profile and focus on building reputation and understanding the ecosystem before expanding.
